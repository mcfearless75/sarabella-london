Before We Meet

Etiquette

Please read before making contact

Step One

Getting in Touch

The best way to reach me is via the enquiry form on this site, or by Twitter DM at @sarabellalondon. I do not respond to unsolicited numbers or blocked calls.

Please introduce yourself properly. Tell me your name, the type of date you have in mind, your preferred dates and times, and — if you have a preference — a hotel or area of London. A well-written first message tells me a great deal about a person and sets the tone for everything that follows.

I am not available for spur-of-the-moment bookings. I ask for advance notice of at least 24 hours for all dates, and longer for overnights, weekends, and travel. Good things are worth planning.

Privacy & Safety

Screening

I screen all new clients. This is non-negotiable and it protects both of us. I ask for a form of ID and, where possible, a reference from a fellow companion or a verifiable professional profile.

Everything you share with me is held in complete confidence. I do not retain unnecessary personal information, I do not share details with third parties, and I handle everything with the same discretion I would want for myself.

If you are uncomfortable with the screening process, we are not a good fit. I make no apologies for that.

Absolute Standard

Discretion

I will never acknowledge a client in public or on social media. I do not discuss the details of our time together with anyone. I keep no records beyond what is necessary to manage my diary safely. My discretion is absolute.

I ask the same in return. Please do not attempt to contact me via personal or professional channels you may become aware of. Do not take photographs during our time together without explicit permission. Do not share details of our dates — however obliquely — anywhere publicly.

Discretion is not a courtesy. It is the foundation of everything I do.

Basic Expectations

Punctuality & Respect

I am always on time. I ask the same of you. If something unavoidable comes up, please let me know as early as possible. Arriving more than 15 minutes late without notice will result in the date being cut short or cancelled with no refund of the deposit.

Personal hygiene is not something I should need to mention, but I will: please arrive clean, groomed, and sober. I enjoy a drink as much as anyone, but I will not see clients who are intoxicated.

I am a person, not a service. Treat me with the same warmth and respect you would expect in return, and we will have an exceptional time together.

Booking Confirmation

Deposits

A deposit is required to confirm all bookings. The amount varies depending on the duration of the date. Details are discussed during the booking process.

Deposits are non-refundable in the event of a cancellation with less than 24 hours' notice. For longer dates — overnights, weekends, and travel — the required notice period and deposit terms are discussed individually.

I understand that genuine emergencies happen. In truly exceptional circumstances, I am reasonable. Repeated last-minute cancellations will result in future bookings being declined.

Important

A Note on Safety

My physical and emotional safety are not negotiable. I reserve the right to end any date at any point if I feel uncomfortable or unsafe. In that event, no refund will be offered.

Please do not attempt to negotiate services that are not offered. Do not attempt to remove or renegotiate agreements made in advance. Pressure of any kind will result in the immediate end of our time together and no future bookings.
