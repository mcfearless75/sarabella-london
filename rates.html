Longer Dates

Dinner Dates, Overnights & Weekends

Longer dates are where the real experience begins. A dinner date — four or five hours together — gives us the time to actually relax into one another's company. We'll eat somewhere excellent, drink something good, and let the evening unfold naturally.

Overnights are available in London or at your hotel. There's something entirely different about waking up without the rush — a slow morning, perhaps breakfast, the unhurried pleasure of having no particular place to be.

Weekend bookings are available and wonderful — two days and two nights in a city you know or somewhere entirely new. I travel light, I adapt beautifully, and I genuinely love exploring somewhere with good company.

For theatre, opera, gallery openings, or corporate events requiring an elegant companion, the five-hour evening rate covers the event, dinner, and private time to follow.
